Dimension Stream

Dimension stream is a start up, focusing on 3D reconstruction using consumer-end hardware. The technology is based on our previous research at the Computer Graphics group at the Chalmers University of Technology. Our vision is to bring you real-time 3D-video (recorded and streaming), and easy acquirement of 3D assets (for example, a body-scanned avatar, using only a mobile camera).
